Welcome to BEPO

A psychological health test and awareness for depression of teenagers - mobile application
Download Learn more

BEPO (Be Positive) is a mental health awareness application that focuses on teenage depression.

The purpose of this application is to help teenagers gain a deep understanding of the importance of having good mental health and how the risk of depression can be managed and prevented.
